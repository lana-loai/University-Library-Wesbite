The Hussein Bin Talal Library offers many services,
some of which are :

  • Borrowing :

    In order to borrow items from our Library, you must present a valid YU ID, or a Subscribed Visitorâ€™s Card issued by the Library.

    There is a time limit to the borrowing periods of each individual; students are allowed 14 days with a limit of 5 items. Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, the library has been cooperating with governmental orders and has extended the borrowing period several times over the course of the past year to make it easier for its visitors to borrow and return items during the pandamic restrictions.

    To return/renewal your borrowing request, you must visit the circulation desk (where one would normally return books) one day prior to the due date.

    If you fail to return the items on the due date, or if you lose/mutilate the items you have borrowed, you will be fined. For more details about this please check the official library website for the latest information on this topic here.
