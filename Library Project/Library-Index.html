Welcome, dear first-year students!

This is a site dedicated to introducing new students to Al-Hussein Bin Talal Library !

Sadly, due to the Covid-19 pandemic restrictions, many new students have yet to experience what Yarmouk University has to offer. From its many faculties and different facilities, to the lovely library that resides in the university, the new-coming students have had their right of exploring the university taken away from them by the sudden and unfortunate global pandemic. This is why we have made this website ! in the hopes that we can let our students have a taste of our library !

Throughout the pages of our site you will find out more and more about our library. Although it certainly isn't the same as visiting in person, we hope this website will be helpful enough until this pandemic passes and you can come and visit us yourself.

Please feel free to share this website with your peers and spread it around !
