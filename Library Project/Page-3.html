Library History :



The Hussein Bin Talal Library was first opened in 1976. It was moved to the new building, which It is a 4-story building with a basement floor, in 2002.

The library building resides between the Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology and the gymnasium building. The Hussein Bin Talal Library can seat about 3000 visiters at a time, and the building is about 18,000 sm in total.

The library is open to authorized individuals, such as students and teaching staff. It is also open to the public, but only authorized individuals may borrow texts/books from the library.
